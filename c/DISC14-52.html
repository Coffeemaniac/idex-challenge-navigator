Problem Statement

integration with the sensors of Intrusion Protection Security

Awareness Picture(CSAP).
1. The proposal is to have an integrated Situational Awareness
(SA) display in form of CSAP at Ground Defence Control
Centre (GDCC). The said integrated picture should be capable of receiving, processing and fusing the inputs from the sensors of CUAS and IPSS. The system should also have the capability of indicating the location of Static/ Patrolling guards and QRT.
These deployed personnel should also have a facility to generate and communicate an alarm, which should be displayed at GDCC with location data.
2. Integration Capability. The system should be capable of integrating inputs from the following:-
2.1. Sensors of CUAS both Passive (RF) and Active Radar.
2.2. Location data of Static/ Mobile/ Patrolling/ QRT personnel
(a module in this regard also needs to be developed)

Challenge Brief / Definition

3. Composite Picture. The picture should be high-resolution and targets picked up by CUAS server be indicated by symbols on a layered map of the layout of the Station. This display should also indicate real time location data of static and mobile guards patrolling parties.
4. Alarm Generation. Facility to generate alarm (both audio and visual) as and when received from guards and on the pickup reported by CUAS Sensors.
5. Zoom In/ Out. The CSAP should have feature of Zoom in/ out without comprising the picture resolution.
6. Layering. The display should have a provision of allowing the user to select/ deselect the layers in the display.
7. Size. The size of display should be not less than 65 inch.
8. No. of Display. The display needs to be replicated with independent control for minimum of two additional users.
9. Remote Display. Facility of extending a display at the S/by location. This may be available either on wireless mode (to be confirmed for its feasibility subject to existing IT regulations
(in IAF) or wired.
10. COTS. Software used/ sub parts/ peripherals/ major/ minor components to be COTS with module based feature to address future additions/ speedy rectification etc.
11. Up-gradation Feature. The system as a whole need to have feature of upgrading in terms of both hardware and software as per the contemporary requirements of CUAS equipment, concept and IT/ CERT requirements.
12. Software. Windows/ Linux based software with up gradation facility having the shelf life of equipment (min 10
Years).
13. Alarm Generation (External). The system to have a feature of generating alarm around various locations in the base both in forming Siren/ pre feed voice messages.
14. GUI. GUI should be Icon driven for each facility for ease of Operations by the user.
