Problem Statement

AI-Driven Propulsion Shaft Alignment and Real-Time Load
Diagnostics for Enhanced Shipbuilding Efficiency

Challenge Brief / Definition

Development of an AI powered software integrated with empirical methods to optimize shaft alignment, minimize stress concentrations and ensure reliable performance. Core technology shall include: High-fidelity Finite Element
Analysis (FEA) to model shaft behaviour under operational loads. Real-time bearing load and vibration monitoring through integrated sensor systems. Utilization of dynamic alignment techniques accounting for thermal growth and hull deflection.
Tentative total INR 0.05 Cr development budget
