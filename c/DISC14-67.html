DISC 14 IAF DISC14-67 PS 67

Problem Statement

Radio is essential alternate media in Network Centric Warfare.
It ensures reliable service in case of failure of optical media i.e.
OFC based communication and provide effective overlay over
OFC based communication infrastructure. Presently Foreign
OEM equipment being used to provide radio communication.

Challenge Brief / Definition

Design and development of High capacity and spectral efficient
Ethernet LOS Radio Equipment to provide effective BW of 1
Gbps or more.
1. Indigenous High capacity Ethernet Radio should provide
1 Gbps or more bandwidth in given standard environment (good weather condition) and support following
1.1 Modulation Techniques: BPSK, QPSK, QAM-4 to QAM-1024, OFDM or latest modulation techniques
1.2 Forward Error Correction: Latest Forward Error
Correction
1.3 Adaptive Code Modulation (ACM)
1.4 Automatic Transmit Power Control (ATPC)
