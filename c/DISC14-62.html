DISC 14 IAF DISC14-62 PS 62

Problem Statement

Mouth of aircraft shelters and sun shelter are open to attack by FPV/ programmable drones. A system is required to prevent imaging/ attack/ disruption of aircraft operations from HAS and sun shelter

Challenge Brief / Definition

1. Design and develop a system to rapidly cover Hardened aircraft shelter and sun shelter.
2. The system should be able to install on existing
Hardened shelters made of concrete and industrial sheds.
3. It should provide automated and remote operation capability.
4. Integrated power supply/battery/ any other technology to work when power disrupted.
5. Opaque/ disruption to IR/ SAR/EO imaging systems.
6. Sufficient strength to prevent damage to aircraft in case of a loitering munition attack on the mouth of aircraft shelter.
7. System to be repairable in parts in case of damage to the covering structure.

Application / Use Case

Will be utilised as a layer of protection against FPV/ programmable drones.
