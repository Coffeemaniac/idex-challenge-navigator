Problem Statement

Development of Graphite Based Wet Multiple Friction Disc for an
Automatic Transmission. Application: OFF-Highway Rear Dump
Truck.

Challenge Brief / Definition

Prior versions like Paper / Elastomer based friction disc element characteristics required performance enhancement at various terrain conditions. To overcome the above technology gap, Graphite based wet multiple discs are identified and globally recognised for performance improvement during various severe operating conditions in an automatic transmission wet multiple clutch engagement mechanism.
Characteristics:
· High load grade having great elasticity.
· Superior resistance to heat and oil.
· Clutch Disc which has high heat resistance co-efficient and high energy absorbing capacity.
· High durability.
