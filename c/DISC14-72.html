DISC 14 IAF DISC14-72 PS 72

Problem Statement

DWDM is an important equipment of Optical Transmission
Network. It ensures reliable service of optical communication.
Present DWDM which is deployed have only 10G BW per lambda insufficient to future requirement of Air Force.

Challenge Brief / Definition

Design and development of Indigenous High capacity DWDM
Equipment to provide effective BW of 100 Gbps or more
Indigenous High capacity DWDM Eqpt should provide 100Gbps or more bandwidth per lambda and support following:-
1. Latest optical Modulation Techniques.
2. Latest Forward Error Correction.
3. Support multirate client interface 1G, 10G, STM1/4/16 etc.
4. System should support multiple lambda.
5. Redundancy at all levels.
