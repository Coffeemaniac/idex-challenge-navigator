DRISHTI Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) DRISHTI-35 PS 35

Problem Statement

Design and development of 0.5 to 40 GHz
Front End Receiver for Airborne
Application.

Challenge Brief / Definition

Design and development of a Wide band
Front End Receiver with Low noise figure less than 10 db
The Front End Receiver has to cover wide band frequency from 0.5 to 40 GHz. The critical parameters are Gain, Noise Figure,
Spurious & Harmonic Levels that need to be maintained uniform response across the frequency band with temperature variation
The product is meant for airborne EW application for various platforms
The product to be complied to Technical specifications and subjected to environmental tests as per MIL STD
810H, MIL STD 461F EMI/EMC and
MIL STD 704D and certification from
CEMILAC to be obtained.

Development Budget

Budget: INR 0.60 Cr
