iDEX Open Challenge iDEX (DIO) OPEN-1 PS 1

Description

The iDEX Open Challenge is a standing invitation to innovators, startups, individuals, and MSMEs to propose defence-relevant technology solutions outside the scope of the regularly posted DISC / ADITI / DRISHTI problem statements. Submissions are accepted any time and evaluated on a rolling basis by iDEX and the Services, covering emerging technologies such as AI/ML, autonomous systems, quantum, communications, new materials, sensors, and more.

About iDEX Open Challenge
